Avocado Feta Salad

Description

This avocado feta salad is a personal favorite. Adjust the feta to taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cubed avocado
  • 0.25 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 0.25 cup diced feta cheese

Steps

  1. Place avocado cubes in a bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, lime juice, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix in feta cheese.
  3. Pour dressing over avocado cubes; toss to coat.
